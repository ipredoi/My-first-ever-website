About Me

Automotive Engineer. School of Code Student. Adventurer

Hi, I am Ionut and I am an Automotive Engineer and SoC student.

I am originally from Bucharest, Romania and now I live in Coventry, United Kingdom.

I moved here three years ago, when I started to work as a Design Engineer for Jaguar Land Rover, the biggest car manufacturer in the UK.

Since childhood, I have been attracted to cars, how things work and video games.

At the age of 7, I got my first video game console, an Ending-Man Terminator which was a Nintendo clone sold in Eastern Europe.

A few years later, my parents bought me my first computer, a very heavy Windows 98 IBM, which opened my world to technology, computer games and the Internet.

During high school, I didn’t find computer programming very attractive and in 2013 I graduated Automotive Engineering at University Politehnica of Bucharest. In 2017 I obtained my Master’s Diploma in Automotive Engineering after 3 years of splitting between a full-time job, courses, seminars, laboratories, projects, family and friends.

In 2013 I started my first full-time job as a Service Advisor in a Mercedes-Benz workshop.

In 2014 I left my service advisor job and I started to work as a Mechanical Engineer for Group Renault-Nissan . After more than three fulfilling years, I took a step forward and followed my dream to work as a Design Engineer for Jaguar Land Rover.

Due to the Covid-19 Pandemic, after three years full of accomplishments and challenges, my journey with JLR came to an end and now, I am very excited to start my journey into tech as a School of Code student.
